ABOUT US

Founded in 1982, RR Pednekar, has been a research focused Architecture & Property Valuation organization. We serve our clients in the most structured & reliable judgments for the real estate sector.

It is a mature practice of experienced professionals as well as young talented Valuers / Architects / Analysts providing quality real estate & property valuation services across India.

In the last 5 years, the company has valued properties totaling more than Rupees 4500 Crores across India, the single highest property valued worth Rupees 650 crores in Mumbai. Our expertise is in ‘Land and Building’ across various types of properties like Hospitality, Industries, Commercial Establishments, Shopping centers, Residential Projects, Public Buildings and others.

This is a reliable practice catering to many eminent corporate houses as well as individuals including top persons in the ministries, government etc. We have formulated scientific approach to value the real estate properties. We care for the clients and their needs; we provide the best methods of valuation which are compliant with banks, financial institutions and government regulations.

Actual fluctuations in the open market are updated in our database to provide our clients with the authentic and latest value of their property.
